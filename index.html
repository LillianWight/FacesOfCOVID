Flozier Tabangin said her husband, Warlito Valdez, was a hero. Valdez died of COVID-19 .... He was 47. His employer, the Richmond Society for Community Living, said it became aware he caught it on the job as a residential worker helping people with intellectual and physical disabilities. "My husband has a very good heart. He is such a gentleman and a very nice person, loving husband. "It's just too sad he's gone." Now, she said, her only priority is how to raise and provide for their young daughter without him.

I lost my best friend to Covid19 a few hours ago. Dr Frank Gabrin was an ER Doctor in NYC. He planned to go back to work when he recovered. It took only five days from the first sign of symptoms. He leaves a husband of less than one year behind. We are devastated.

Juanita and Howard Robinson's romance started with double dates ... and ended more than 65 years later as they held hands on their final day together. Juanita, 91, died at 8 p.m. on April 6. Five hours later, Howard died. He was 94. "It's so easy to say, 'Oh, those people were old, they would have died anyway,'" the couple's second daughter, Diana Coleman, said. "But they still added value to everyone's life around them, not to mention their own family. "They still had a lot to give and that was taken away from them."

Her family hopes that sharing her story makes others think of real people — seniors who are isolated from their families during the pandemic — instead of numbers when statistics are released on COVID-19 cases and deaths. It also gives them a chance to talk about all the things they loved about Margaret Shea, from her stylish glasses to how she'd wipe the floor with any man who dared to step on a golf course with her, at a time when physical distancing makes a celebration of life ceremony impossible.

They were out on the concourse by the ticket office when they were assaulted by a member of the public who spat at them ... and told them he had the virus.... Within days of the assault, both women fell ill with the virus.... Belly died ... 14 days after she was assaulted at Victoria Station. The last time Belly’s husband saw her was when she was taken away in the ambulance.
